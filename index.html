What We Do We Excel at This

M&A

We are an integrated partner to our clients in the origination and execution of their strategic vision via M&A and for beyond that. We provide top-notch services on buy and sell side, as well as strategic, structured, and cross-border M&A.

Buy side

On buy side, we support our clients in every step of M&A target search, deal making, and post-deal integration, including:

  • target and its alternatives identification, valuation, risks assessment, synergies consideration, deal financing options
  • negotiations, structuring, cross-boarder support
  • strategic M&A - all M&A opportunities, in and beyond client's sector/domain, including sector consolidation, entering new market/domain/niche via acquisition of a local target etc

Sell side

On sell side, we support on every stage of a M&A transaction, from the sale decision to the actual deal as well as any post-transactional period, including:

  • vendor dual diligence that rigorously assesses investment viability and business attractiveness, including for a specific buyer
  • sale and exit strategy that enhances the company appeal to potential buyers and investors
  • capital structuring - the design of the optimal equity and debt structures to facilitate M&A transaction
  • advise on strategic alternatives, such as joint ventures, recapitalizations, spin-offs/carve-outs/demergers, and other complex transactions

Financing

Our approach and execution bring judgment to our clients’ financing and liquidity needs — from trivial financing issuance to the most complex transactions.

Equity

We advise on, originate, structure, and execute equity and equity-related transactions and solutions, including:

  • equity from leading European, USA, and world private equity funds
  • private placements, derivatives
  • venture capital, fund of funds, angel equity
  • evaluation of proposed IPOs, pre-IPO planning, equity story, valuation and selection of bookrunners
  • initial public offerings, follow-on offerings, and convertible bonds.

Debt

We originate, structure, market, and execute debt-related financings, including:

  • banking, capital markets and project financing
  • capital structure advisory on debt financing, including credit ratings, optimal capital structure, liability management, and capital return
  • direct access to different debt providers: local/international commercial/investment banks, bond investors, mezzanine debt providers etc

Strategic Advisory
Corporate Finance

We advise clients on corporate finance matters, including:

  • company/asset valuation, pre-deal analysis
  • growth/capitalization strategy - new market, M&A, mergers, JVs etc
  • market/industries research, entering new industries/domains/countries
  • tailored advices/solutions - to enhance shareholders value, meet specific client needs

Restructuring

We support clients in restructuring deals, including:

  • advisory services to stakeholders (debtors/lenders)
  • tailored restructuring options
  • refinancing strategy, covenants renegotiation, debt-to-equity swap
  • stakeholders negotiation

Board Advisory

We assist our clients as member of their boards on different matters, including:

  • guidance and supervision of investment strategy
  • company strategy development and its implementation for one, two, more years
  • innovation/digitalization strategy
  • top-management hunting and mentoring

Distressed
Distressed Assets

We advice our clients on situations that require special attention and business/investment creativity, including:

  • distressed assets - not performing assets, not meeting financial targets
  • distressed acquisitions and disposals
  • uniques situations, shareholders/stakeholders not able to reach business decisions etc
  • complex situations, such as insolvency, financial underperformance
